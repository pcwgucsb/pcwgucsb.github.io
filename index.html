About the Group

The UCSB Political Communication Working Group brings together faculty and graduate students from across the UCSB campus who study big questions in contemporary political communication. This interdisciplinary group meets regularly to share feedback on ongoing research, discuss emerging issues in the field, and to foster collaborative projects.

Our goal is to build and sustain a vibrant community of political communication scholars at UC Santa Barbara.
